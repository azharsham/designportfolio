Study Details
Project Background
Were creating a Villa Maya Table Reservation to attract and retain customers in
our online system. We noticed that our competitors offer dedicated mobile apps
for their customers to book tables , and they have been very successful. We
want to create a product that can compete in the market, improve sales, and
increase customer satisfaction.
Research Questions
1. How long does it take for a
user book the table using the
app?
2. Are users able to successfully
book the table for the date and
time they want ?
3. What can we learn from the
steps users took book the
table ?
4. Are there any parts of the table
booking process where users
are getting stuck?
5. Is the confirmation process
easy for the customer?
Participants
5 participants
Participants between the ages
of 18-62.
Methodology
25-30 minutes
India, remote
Unmoderated usability study)
Users were asked to book the
table using the app
